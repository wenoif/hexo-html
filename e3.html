英语语法3-从句分类-形容词从句-名词从句-副词从句

发表于2021-03-26|英语
|字数总计:3.3k|阅读时长:13分钟|阅读量:

从句分类

除了谓语动词之外的句子成分都可以充当从句，根据词性也可以给从句分类。

  1. 名词从句：主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句、宾语补语从句、同位语从句；
  2. 形容词从句：定语从句（关系从句）；形容词从句=定语从句=关系从句；
  3. 副词从句：状语从句（[时间/地点/条件/原因/方式/比较]状语从句）；

形容词从句

形容词从句作用是限定范围或性质

从句结构：
a carrot that I bought.
先行词：“carrot”；
关系词：“that”；
[定语/形容词/关系]从句：“that I bought”。

关系代词：关系词起到了“代词的作用“

关系从句 陈述句 陈述句语序，含关系代词 从句
The rabbit is eating a carrot that I bought. I bought the carrot. I bought that. that I bought.
The teacher whom I saw yesterday is a rabbit. I saw the teacher yesterday I saw whom yesterday. whom I saw yesterday.
The food which the teacher likes is a carrot. The teacher likes the food. The teacher likes which. which the teacher likes.

that VS which
先行词是唯一的使用that: The first rabbit that ate a carrot. “first”起到限定作用，只能是”first“，没有其他选择，所以使用that；
先行词是有选择的which: The rabbit ate a carrot which I bought.兔子吃胡萝卜吃的哪一根？吃的我买的那一根，还有可能不是我买的胡萝卜，还有其他选择，所以使用which。

关系副词：
This was the place where the rabbit ate the carrot.这就是兔子吃胡萝卜的地方。”where”修饰”place“，起到的作用不是代词，而是副词作用。

关系副词转关系代词：关系副词=”介词+关系代词“
This was the place at which the rabbit ate the carrot.这就是兔子吃胡萝卜的地方。

非限定性形容词从句是指”没有限定范围或性质“的从句
The rabbit ate the carrot,the carrot was on the table.兔子吃了胡萝卜，胡萝卜在桌子上。
The rabbit ate the carrot,which was on the table.兔子吃了桌子上的胡萝卜。

名词从句

主要就三类：that引导、whether/if引导、特殊疑问词引导。

主语从句

主语从句就是作为主语的从句。

The fact is obvious 这事实是明显的.
主语是”The fact“，
将主语换成一个句子”That the rabbit ate a carrot“
得到主语从句”That the rabbit ate a carrot is obvious“，
”That“是从属连词（引导词），表明这是一个that引导的主语从句。

形式主语：
That引导的主语从句：That the hungry rabbit had already eaten a carrot for dinner this evening is obvious.饥饿的兔子今晚晚餐已经吃了胡萝卜，这是显而易见的。
改成形式主语（It本身作为主语没有意义，是个形式而已，指代后面的从句作为主语）：It is obvious that the hungry rabbit had already eaten a carrot for dinner this evening.很明显，饥饿的兔子今晚晚餐已经吃了一个胡萝卜。

特殊疑问词引导的从句 形式主语
That the rabbit ate a carrot is obvious. It is obvious that the rabbit ate a carrot.
Whether the rabbit ate a carrot is obvious. It is obvious whether the rabbit ate a carrot.
Where the rabbit ate a carrot is obvious. It is obvious where the rabbit ate a carrot.
When the rabbit ate a carrot is obvious. It is obvious when the rabbit ate a carrot.
How the rabbit ate a carrot is obvious. It is obvious how the rabbit ate a carrot.
Who ate the carrot is obvious. It is obvious who ate the carrot.
What the rabbit ate is obvious. It is obvious what the rabbit ate.

宾语从句

宾语从句就是从句在整个复杂句种做宾语，可能包含”确定信息“也可能包含”不确定信息“。
确定信息作宾语从句：I saw that the rabbit ate the carrot.
不确定信息作宾语从句(不确定信息就是“何时”、“何地”、“如何”等)：
I saw whether the rabbit ate the carrot.
I saw who the rabbit ate the carrot.
I saw what the rabbit ate the carrot.
I saw where the rabbit ate the carrot.

引导词that可以省略但有时不可省略,其他引导词不可省略：
I know (that) the rabbit ate a carrot.主语动词”know“，谓语动词”ate“；
that不可省略情况：
I didn’t know (that) the rabbit ate the carrot and that you saw it.

否定加在谓语动词之前，而非从句中：
I don’t think (that) the rabbit is smart. 而不是 I think (that) the rabbit isn’t smart.

主句从句时态一致：
I don’t think (that) you are right.
I didn’t think (that) you were right.

表语从句

什么是表语？系动词之后赋值给系动词前的主语。表语=主语补语。
表语从句：用一个从句充当系动词之后的表语，常见系动词是be动词。

主语从句–>表语从句
*That the rabbit ate a carrot*** is a secret.
The secret is *that the rabbit ate a carrot.***

系动词不是be动词，还可能是feel、seem、look、taste等
It seems that the rabbit is hungry.其中的”It“没有意义，意义在表语从句中。
It feels that you’re not telling the truth.

同位语从句

同位语就是用不同的方式把主语再说一遍。
当一个从句充当同位语时就是同位语从句。

The fact that the rabbit ate the carrot did not surprise me.兔子吃胡萝卜的事实并没有使我吃惊。”兔子吃胡萝卜” “事实”是同位语，使用that引导。
The question whether the rabbit will eat the carrot is on all our minds.兔子是否会吃胡萝卜的问题一直萦绕在我们的心头。

“be+形容词+从句”的分类有所争议，例如I am sure that there’s a carrot on the table.
同位语从句为：I am sure of the fact that there’s a carrot on the table.

宾语补语从句

You can call me Papa Rabbit. 宾语”me“；宾语补语”Papa Rabbit“；
当一个从句充当宾语补语时就是宾语补语从句。
My education made me who I am today.宾语”me“，宾语补语从句”who I am today“；

副词从句

起到补充说明的作用，充当了状语的作用，所以”副词从句=状语从句“。
[条件/让步/方式]副词从句：都是阐述某种情况/做出某种假设。
[原因/目的/结果]副词从句：都表达了某种因果，A导致B的情况。

时间状语从句

状态 引导词 英例 汉例
在某一时间点之前 before 主句+从句：The rabbit ate a carrot at home before the wolf stopped by.

从句+逗号，主句：Before the wolf stopped by , the rabbit ate a carrot at home.		 在狼来之前，兔子正在家里吃胡萝卜。

狼来之前，兔子在家里吃了一根胡萝卜。
在某一时间点之中 when（时间点，表突然）、
while（时间段）、
as（同时进行）		 The rabbit was eating a carrot when I reached home.
The rabbit was eating a carrot while I was making a video.
I ate a carrot as I made the video.		 我到家时，兔子正在吃胡萝卜。
当我在做视频的时候，兔子正在吃胡萝卜。
我一边做视频一边吃胡萝卜。
在某一时间点之后 after The rabbit ate a carrot after the wolf stopped by. 狼路过之后，兔子吃了一根胡萝卜。
从之前某一时间点开始算 since，使用完成状态 The rabbit has shared three carrots since the wolf stopped by.
The rabbit has not stopped sharing carrots since the wolf stopped by.
直到之后某一时间点 until The rabbit waited until the wolf stopped by.
The rabbit didn’t eat any carrot until the wolf stopped by.		 兔子一直等到狼停来了。
直到狼来了兔子才吃胡萝卜。
一…就… as soon as The rabbit will eat a carrot as soon as the wolf leaves.
下次… the next time The rabbit will share the biggest carrot the next time the wolf stops by.

地点状语从句

引导词：where + 强调形式（wherever、everywhere、anywhere）

地点副词从句：The rabbit ate the carrot where he found it.（”where“直接指地点）
引导定语从句：The rabbit ate the carrot at the place where he found it.（”where“修饰先行词”place“）
引导表语从句：This is where he found the carrot.
引导主语从句：Where he found the carrot is a secret.

强调形式：wherever无论哪里、everywhere所有地方、anywhere任何地方
The rabbit will go where he can find a carrot.
The rabbit will go wherever he can find a carrot.
The rabbit sees a carrot everywhere (every place where 定语从句) he goes.
The rabbit sees a carrot anywhere (at any place where 定语从句) he goes.

比较状语从句

英语形容词的比较级：smart、smarter；big、bigger；beautiful、more beautiful；

比较方式 比较词 例
更…… than He is smarter than I am.
This carrot is bigger than that one is.
She is more beautiful than Snow White is.
同样….. as ….. as He is as smart as I am.
This carrot is as big as that one is.
She is as beautiful as Snow White is.
越….越…. the （比较级），the （比较级） The more you practice English,the more fluent you become.
The more coins you give me,the more videos I make.

条件状语从句

即条件句，引导词：if、unless、provided、as long as、in case。
主将从现：从句”一般（现在）时态“、主句”将来时态“。即从句比主句落后一个时间段。

引导词：if
真实条件句：If the rabbit sees a carrot,he will eat it.
虚拟条件句(和事实相反)：If I saw a carrot,I would eat it.

引导词：unless
If you like this video,you will give it a thumbs-up;
If you don’t like this video,you will not give it a thumbs-up;
Unless you like this video,you will not give it a thumbs-up;

其他引导词：provided、as long as、in case
Provided that my video is excellent,you will give it a thumbs-up;
As long as I make excellent videos,you will remain my follower.
In case you don’t understand,please comment below.

让步状语从句

阐述”相反/相对“的情况。即使/就算….也…。引导词：even、although、even though（不能使用but，为什么？）、no matter（可以使用”regardless of“取代）、

让步副词从句（假设）：Even if I see a carrot,I will not eat it.
让步副词从句（事实）：Although if I see a carrot,I will not eat it.
条件副词从句：If I saw a carrot,I would it eat it.

No matter what happens,I will not eat carrot.
Regardless of what happens,I will not eat carrot.

方式状语从句

引导词：as

表示好像某事发生过的方式：
方式副词从句：I feel good as if I just ate a carrot.我感觉好极了，好像刚吃了一根胡萝卜。（主句”I feel good“是已经发生了的事实，”I just ate a carrot“就像发生了一样。）
条件副词从句：If I had eaten a carrot,I would feel good.如果我吃了胡萝卜，我会感觉很好。（表假设）
方式副词从句：You spend so much money as if you were a millionaire,你花了那么多钱就好像你是百万富翁一样。（主句”You spend so much money“已经发生，”you were a millionaire“就像发生过一样。）
虚拟语气：If you were a millionaire,you would spend a lot of money.如果你是百万富翁，你会花很多钱。
形容词从句：You spend money the way in which millionaires do.

好像某事正在发生的方式：
方式副词从句：Eat the carrot as I do.像我一样吃胡萝卜。
形容词从句：Eat the carrot the way in which I eat it.按我吃胡萝卜的方式吃。
方式副词从句：Leave the carrot as it is.让胡萝卜保持原样。

原因状语从句

引导词：because、since、as；
语气区别：because表达原因 > since表达众所周知 > as表达显而易见；
语序区别：because引导从句可以放在主句之前或之后，since或as引导从句只能放在主句之前。
英语中没有”Because……so…….“语句，为什么？because是构词原因副词从句的引导词、so是构成复合句的连词。

The rabbit ate the carrot because he was hungry.兔子吃胡萝卜是因为他饿了原因副词从句”because he was hungry.“。
Since you are hungry,you can eat the carrot,既然你饿了，你可以吃胡萝卜
As you were not here,we ate the carrot with out you.因为你不在这里，我们没有带你一起吃胡萝卜。

Because VS for
Because you like me,you have given all my videos a thumbs-up;因为你喜欢我[原因]，你对我所有的视频都竖起了大拇指[结果]；
You must really like me,for you have given all my videos a thumbs-up.你一定很喜欢我[推测结果]，因为你对我所有的视频都竖起了大拇指[推测原因]。”You must really like me“和”you have given all my videos a thumbs-up“具有相同地位，for作为一个连词。

目的状语从句

为了达到A（从句），做了事件B（主句），常常使用情态动词：can、could、may、might、should、would等
In order that I could finish the video in time[从句],I pulled an all-night[主句].为了能及时拍完视频，我熬了一个通宵。

结果状语从句

作用是解释说明主句的情况，主句所产生的结果，引导词：so that（so后面接形容词）
The temperature was low so that the lake froze.温度很低，所以湖都结冰了。

so that 和 so…..that
I ate a lot of carrots for lunch so that I wasn’t hungry at all in the afternoon.我午饭吃了很多胡萝卜，所以下午一点也不饿。
I ate so many carrots for lunch that I felt sick afterwards.我午饭吃了那么多胡萝卜，后来觉得不舒服。

so that VS so
I like this video,so I’ll give it a thumbs-up.”I like this video“和”I’ll give it a thumbs-up.“是两个同等的句子，so连接两个并列句。
